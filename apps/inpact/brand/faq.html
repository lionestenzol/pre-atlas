Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just another productivity app?

No. in-PACT is a personal transformation framework powered by behavioral science and AI. It helps you align your life with your values, purpose, and long-term goals—not just manage tasks.

What if I'm not a "high achiever"?

You don't have to be. in-PACT is for anyone who wants to be more intentional, grounded, and aligned. Whether you're just starting out or running a team, the system adapts to your level.

How is this different from journaling or therapy?

While those tools are valuable, in-PACT provides structure, assessment, and actionable feedback. It combines AI-driven analysis with personalized planning to turn reflection into real change.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You’re free to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan whenever you need—no questions asked.
