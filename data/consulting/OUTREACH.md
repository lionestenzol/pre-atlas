# Outreach Playbook

## Target List Criteria

**Who to reach out to:**

| Criteria | Details |
|---|---|
| **Company size** | 50-500 employees |
| **Industries** | E-commerce, B2B SaaS, professional services, logistics, healthcare admin |
| **Titles** | CEO, COO, VP Operations, Head of Customer Success, RevOps Manager |
| **Signals** | Hiring for ops/admin roles, complaining about manual work on LinkedIn, using 5+ SaaS tools, growing fast (Series A-C) |
| **Budget indicator** | Already paying for Zapier/Make/automation tools, or spending on contractors for ops work |

**Where to find them:**
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (filter by company size + title + industry)
- Local business networks and chambers of commerce
- Slack communities (SaaS, e-commerce, ops-focused)
- Twitter/X (founders complaining about ops)
- Referrals from existing contacts

I build AI automation systems that eliminate manual workflows — things like ticket routing, data entry, lead qualification, and internal approvals. My last project automated 12 daily operations that used to require manual intervention every morning.

Would a 30-minute call to see if there's a fit make sense this week?

[YOUR NAME]

## Discovery Call Script (30 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time. I'll keep this focused — my goal is to understand what's eating your team's time and whether automation can help. If it can, I'll tell you exactly how. If it can't, I'll tell you that too."

### Pain Identification (10 min)

1. "Walk me through your team's typical day — what are the first 3 things they do when they log in?"
2. "Which of those tasks do you wish didn't require a human?"
3. "How many hours per week does your team spend on repetitive manual work? Rough estimate is fine."
4. "What tools does your team use daily?" (CRM, email, Slack, spreadsheets, etc.)
5. "Have you tried automating any of this before? What happened?"

### Quantification (5 min)

6. "If you could eliminate [SPECIFIC TASK they mentioned], how many hours per week would that save?"
7. "What's the cost of that time? Roughly — salary, opportunity cost, errors?"
8. "Is this getting worse as you grow, or staying flat?"

### Budget & Timeline (5 min)

9. "If I showed you a working system that handles [SPECIFIC TASK] in 2 weeks, what would that be worth to your business?"
10. "Do you have budget allocated for operations improvement, or would this need approval?"

### Close (8 min)

**If qualified:**
"Here's what I'd recommend. We start with a $1,500 Automation Audit — I spend 3 hours mapping your operations, identify the top opportunities, and give you a roadmap with ROI estimates. You'll know exactly what to automate first and what it'll save. From there, if the numbers make sense, we can do a 2-week paid pilot on the highest-impact item. Want me to send the details?"

**If not qualified:**
"Honestly, based on what you've described, I don't think automation is the right move right now. [REASON]. When [CONDITION], that's when it makes sense to revisit. I appreciate your time."

## Outreach Cadence

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | Send cold email |
| Day 3 | Connect on LinkedIn (no message) |
| Day 5 | LinkedIn DM if they accepted, or follow-up email if not |
| Day 10 | Final follow-up email |
| Day 14 | Move to nurture list (engage with their content monthly) |

**Volume target:** 5 new prospects per day = 25 per week = 100 per month

**Expected conversion:**
- 100 outreach → 10-15 replies → 5-8 calls → 2-3 audits → 1 pilot

That's 1 paying client per month from cold outreach alone. Network referrals will accelerate this.
