## Extracted Frameworks

### Conversational Priming Protocol
How to extract maximum value from AI interactions:
1. **Small talk phase** — establish rapport, give context about yourself and your situation
2. **Hint seeding** — drop partial ideas without full specification, see if the AI extends them in the direction you intended
3. **Validation loop** — if it goes where you wanted, you confirm and deepen; if not, you redirect with another hint
4. **Business phase** — once the AI understands your context and direction, shift to execution mode
5. **Self-reflection byproduct** — the act of explaining your situation to the AI forces you to organize your own thinking

Key insight: "You're giving it your situation, which you think you're giving it for it, but you're really doing it for you" — the priming process is bilateral.

### Multi-Model Orchestration (The Roster)
Different AI models have different strengths — treat them as a managed team:
- ChatGPT: ideation, conversation, context retention, emotional processing
- DeepSeek: code analysis, technical implementation, structural thinking
- Workflow: start conversation in one, copy key outputs to another, synthesize across both
- "These are not just steps, these are conversations" — each model interaction is a dialogue, not a transaction
- The orchestrator (you) holds the vision; the models hold the capabilities

### Adversity-as-Constant Pattern
Across every environment (Domino's, Amazon, school, relationships):
- Tested, bullied, or pushed at every job
- Made friends AND got bullshit at every job simultaneously
- External chaos compounds (two jobs + school + relationship + stalker Uber driver)
- "It takes a very special person to go through all these" — not self-pity, but recognition that the density of adversity is abnormal
- Response pattern: make it fun, outwork everyone, leave when the cost exceeds the value
