## Key Concepts Covered

### CRM Lifecycle
- Customer acquisition → retention → loyalty loop
- Touchpoint-based relationship building (brochure → service → survey → follow-up)
- Third-party websites used for monitoring brand reputation and customer feedback, not direct sales forecasting

### Sales Analytics
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) as the core predictive metric for prioritizing accounts
- CRM analytics for segment identification and pipeline management
- Data-driven quota setting vs. gut-based targets

### Sales Force Organization
- **Product structure** — when products require heavy specialization (B2B tech with diverse solutions)
- **Customer structure** — when client relationships matter more than product knowledge
- **Geographic structure** — when coverage/territory efficiency is the priority
- **Functional structure** — when sales process stages need dedicated specialists

### Sales Training
- Product knowledge training vs. selling skills training — different design, different timing
- New hire onboarding focuses on product/company knowledge first
- Ongoing training focuses on skill refinement and market changes

### Compensation Models
- Base salary + commission (hybrid) — most common
- Draw against commission — advances against future earnings
- Straight commission — high risk/high reward, best for experienced reps
- Bonus structures tied to team vs. individual performance
